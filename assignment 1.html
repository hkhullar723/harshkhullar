Introduction

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro also known as CR7 and Ronaldo was born on 5 february, 1985 in Sao Pedro parish in Funchal, Madeira Island, Portugal. He is one of the most successful football players of all time. One of the world's most marketable and famous athletes, Ronaldo was ranked the world's highest-paid athlete by Forbes in 2016, 2017 and 2023, and the world's most famous athlete by ESPN from 2016 to 2019.(Wikipedia)

Early childhood

Ronaldo was born in a very poor family. He is the fourth and youngest child of Maria Dolores dos Santos Viveiros da Aveiro, a cook, and José Dinis Aveiro, a municipal gardener and part-time kit man. His mother revealed that she wanted to abort him due to poverty, his father's alcoholism and having too many children already but her doctor refused to perform the procedure, as abortions were illegal in Portugal at that time. By age 14, Ronaldo agreed with his mother to cease his education to focus entirely on football. One year later, he was diagnosed with tachycardia. Ronaldo underwent heart surgery. He was discharged from the hospital hours after the procedure and resumed training a few days later.(Wikipedia)

Club Career

As a child, Ronaldo played for Andorinha from 1992 to 1995, where his father was the kit man and later spent two years with Nacional. At age 16, Ronaldo was promoted from Sporting's youth team by first-team manager lazló Bölöni, who was impressed with his dribbling. 29 September 2002 , Ronaldo made his debut in the Primeira Liga also known as premier league, against Braga and later on his performance in forthcoming matches catched every big team managers eyes.Ronaldo's move to Manchester United was completed on 12 August 2003 and made him the first portugese player to sign for the club. He had a spectacular time period in Manchester United from 2003 to 2009 and then made a transfer to Real Madrid worth of 100 million US dollars.(Wikipedia)

Achievements

Ronaldo has won five Ballon d'Or awards, a record three UEFA Best Player in Europe, and four European Golden Shoes, the most by a European player. He has won 33 trophies in his career, including seven league titles, five UEFA Champions Leagues, the UEFA European Championship and the UEFA Nations League. Ronaldo holds the records for most appearances (183), goals (140) and assists (42) in the Champions League, goals in the European Championship (14), international goals (123) and international appearances (201). He is one of the few players to have made over 1,100 professional career appearances, and has scored over 850 official senior career goals for club and country, making him the highest goalscorer of all time.
